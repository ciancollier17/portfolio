About Me

Hello my name is Cian Collier, I am a web developer from Buckinghamshire in the UK. Currently I mainly work with JavaScript (including modern frameworks and standards such as ES6 and React.JS) but am also interested in working with other programming languages such as PHP and Octave. Present projects include an E-commerce site and an neural network based approach to identifying butterflies with machine learning.

if you like what yuo see on this portfoliio and think I would be a good fit for your project please get in touch via the consultation booking form below and I will forward you a quote.
